about me

My name is Richard Święch. I started learning piano when I was six, in a private musich school. I continued my education with great teachers and piano players such as Piotr Kalicki, Adam Niedzielin, and also at Krakowska Szkoła Jazzu i Muzyki Rozrywkowej. Since the day when I started playing, I’ve gained 25 years of experience in playing piano on many fields, such as solo piano, piano and keyboards in a band, music theory, composition and music production.

At the moment I've got also 15 years of experience in teaching piano. I started sharing my knowledge when I was 18. In 2012 I launched my own private music school - Smileflow ArtCompany - which is now one of the biggest companies here in Cracow, offering private piano, violin and vocal classes.

As a piano teacher I worked with all kinds of people - employees of high-tech companies such as Google and Cisco (including lessons in headquaters of this companies in Cracow), and people who graduated only from high school. Architects, lecturers, nurses, gardeners, whole families, children from age 4-5 through students and adults up to age 60+ (my oldest student was 72 when he started his lessons), people who had 10 or more years of experience, and those who dreamt about playing piano and just wanted to start without knowing anything.
